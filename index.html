FAQ

Questions you might be wondering.

Do you use WordPress or any page builder?
+
No — every website I build is 100% custom HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I never use WordPress, Wix, Squarespace, or any template. This means your site is faster, more unique, and impossible to replicate.
How do international payments work?
+
I accept payments in USD via PayPal, Wise, or bank transfer. I require 50% upfront to start work, and the remaining 50% when the project is complete and you're satisfied. Simple and safe for both sides.
What if I'm not satisfied with the result?
+
Every project includes 2 free revision rounds — I keep refining until you're 100% happy. I've never had a client leave unsatisfied. Your success is my priority, not just delivering a file.
How long does a website take to build?
+
Landing pages: 3–5 days. Restaurant websites: 7–10 days. Luxury premium sites: 10–14 days. These timelines depend on how quickly you provide content (photos, text, menu). Rush delivery is available for urgent projects.
Will my website show up on Google?
+
Yes. Every website I build includes on-page SEO, structured data markup (JSON-LD), Google Maps integration, and fast loading speeds. I also set up Google Analytics so you can track your traffic from day one.
